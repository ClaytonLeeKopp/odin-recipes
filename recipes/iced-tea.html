Lightly Sweetened Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 8 bags Lipton Tea
  • Hot water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • ice

Directions

  1. Place tea bags in 2 quart serving container and fill 3/4 full with hot water
  2. Let tea steep at least 10 minutes
  3. Remove and discard tea bags
  4. Add sugar and stir till dissolved
  5. Add ice until 2 quart container is full
  6. Refrigerate 2 hours
  7. Serve over ice
