Homemade Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 32 oz Swanson's chicken Broth
  • 1 frozen chicken
  • 3 large carrots
  • 3 stalks of celery)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder

Directions

  1. Boil frozen chicken for 1 hour
  2. Remove chicken, set aside 2 cups of stock, discard remaining broth
  3. Carefully remove meat from bones and chop to morsel size as desired
  4. Set meat and stock aside
  5. Slice carrots and Celery into medium-thin slices (1/4in or less)
  6. Pour Swanson's broth, 2 cups stock, and 1 cup water into large pot
  7. Bring pot to boil, then simmer on low heat
  8. Add salt, pepper, chili powder, and vegetables
  9. Cook on low for 30 minutes or until desired firmness of vegetables/li>
  10. Serve, wait 2 minutes to cool
