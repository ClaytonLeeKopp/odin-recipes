Pizza

Ingredients

  • frozen crust
  • 1/2 cup Rao's pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package Tony's sliced pepperoni (about 50 slices)

Directions

  1. Put frozen crust in refrigerator to thaw overnight
  2. Preheat Oven to 400F
  3. Place thawed crust onto pizza pan
  4. Pour sauce onto crust and distribute evenly with a spoon
  5. Spread cheese evenly over surface
  6. Distribute pepperoni slices as desired
  7. Put into preheated oven and cook for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is light brown
  8. When done, remove from oven and cool for 5 minutes
  9. slice and serve
